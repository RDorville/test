Hi there!. My name is Rodney and I am facilitating the option module EP1000 Digital Fabrication and prototyping. In this module we will look at how to make objects on your computer before your come to the workshop/laboratory to fabricate the object. This allows you to design, view, test your object on a digital device before physically fabricating it.

This is the way of the new world!
